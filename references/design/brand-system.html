01 · Rules that bind

  • Exactly three material families: kraft+foil (narrates), dark-roast UI (proves), brass (operates). A fourth is a design bug.
  • Evidence ratio: at least half of every viewport is real product UI or real numbers. Fiction frames, never fills.
  • Honesty badges are load-bearing: ✅ only for deployed capability; 🔜 always carries its epic tag.
  • Every joke sits beside a checkable claim. Footnotes are a design element (class .note), not fine print.
  • Max one hero brass instrument per section.
  • Two CTAs everywhere: Add to Claude (primary) + waitlist (secondary, until E2 flips it to Start free).
  • Type: Archivo (wdth 62–125) + IBM Plex Mono. Two families, no more.
